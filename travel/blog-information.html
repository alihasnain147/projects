The Taj Mahal is a mausoleum, which is a type of building that is used as a tomb. It has a main dome and four minarets, which are tall, narrow towers that are often found in Islamic architecture. The minarets of the Taj Mahal are made of red sandstone and rise to a height of 40 meters (131 feet) above the ground. They were built at an angle so that if they were to fall, they would not hit the main dome and damage the mausoleum. The minarets are also decorated with intricate carvings and calligraphy. Visitors to the Taj Mahal can walk around the monument and see the minarets up close, but they are not allowed to climb them.It is also regarded as a symbol of love and devotion, and is a popular destination for tourists from around the world.The Taj Mahal has undergone several restoration projects over the years to maintain its beauty and structural integrity. The most recent restoration project was completed in 2021 and involved cleaning the marble facade and restoring the intricate inlay work.

The Taj Mahal is a masterpiece of Mughal architecture, combining elements of Islamic, Persian, and Indian styles. The mausoleum is made of white marble and is adorned with intricate carvings and precious stones.The Taj Mahal is considered one of the most beautiful buildings in the world and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

The monument is widely considered to be one of the most beautiful buildings in the world, renowned for its exquisite architecture, intricate carvings, and ornate details. The structure is a combination of Indian, Persian, and Islamic architectural styles, and features a large central dome and four minarets. The Taj Mahal is surrounded by a lush garden, with reflecting pools and fountains. The building is built entirely of white marble and is decorated with precious stones and intricate designs.
